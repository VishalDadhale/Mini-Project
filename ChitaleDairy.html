Navjivan Dairy was founded by the great visionary,Late Shri Bhaskar Ganesh alias Babasaheb Chande (B. G. Chande). A journey that began in 1939, in the small town of Bhilawadi set the stage for a revolution in the country's dairy industry. Babasaheb, as he was affectionately called, set out on an uphill task – to empower local farmers through an enterprise movement. He began with a small milk unit in his own backyard, with the help of fellow farmers.
